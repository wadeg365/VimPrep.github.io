Focus & Priorities

As the VP of Analytics, Julianna's primary concern will be the impact of the migration on Vimeo's analytics capabilities and business intelligence reporting. Her extensive experience in web analytics and business intelligence means she will be evaluating how the new data platform will empower her team to deliver insights to senior executives and support strategic initiatives.

She will be interested in the tools and processes for data access, dashboard development, and the overall ability to derive business value from the migrated data.

Key Insight

Julianna's experience includes leading analytics teams, developing data models, creating dashboards, and working with various data technologies and BI tools. Her focus on providing data support and briefing senior executives highlights her need for a stable and reliable analytics platform.

Watching Out For

  • The availability and accessibility of data in the new BigQuery Lakehouse environment for her analytics teams.
  • The compatibility of existing BI and visualization tools (like Tableau, which she has experience with) with BigQuery.
  • The impact on existing dashboards and reporting workflows and the level of effort required to adapt them to the new platform.
  • The data modeling and data engineering aspects that underpin the analytics layer.
  • The data governance and data quality measures to ensure the reliability of analytics and reporting.
  • How the migration will enable more advanced analytics and data science initiatives (given her previous partnership with data scientists).

Potential Questions

"How will our existing analytics tools integrate with the BigQuery Lakehouse, and what level of effort should we anticipate for adapting our current dashboards and reports?"

Focus on BigQuery's seamless integration with major BI tools, including Tableau. Provide specific examples of connector functionality, discuss minimal adaptation requirements, and note where native BigQuery features might enhance reporting capabilities.

"What data modeling approaches are being proposed for BigQuery to ensure efficient and performant analytics?"

Explain BigQuery's columnar storage, the denormalization approach recommended for analytical workloads, and how materialized views and BI Engine can optimize performance for reporting needs.

"Can you outline the data governance framework for the new platform and how it will ensure the accuracy and reliability of our data for reporting?"

Detail the integrated data quality checks, metadata management capabilities, and data lineage tracking available in BigQuery and connected GCP services. Emphasize how these improve governance compared to the current environment.

"How will this migration potentially enhance our ability to perform more advanced analytics, such as customer segmentation or attribution modeling?"

Highlight BigQuery ML capabilities, integration with other Google Cloud AI services, and the benefits of having a unified data platform for combining different data types (video metadata, user behavior, etc.) for advanced analytics.

"What kind of training and support will be provided to our analytics teams to effectively utilize the new BigQuery environment?"

Present a comprehensive training plan including hands-on workshops, documentation, side-by-side support during transition, and ongoing expert consulting to ensure her team can fully leverage the new capabilities.

Individual Wins

  • A more robust and scalable data platform that can handle increasing data volumes and complexity, leading to more reliable and comprehensive analytics.
  • Improved data accessibility and performance for her analytics teams, enabling them to deliver insights more efficiently.
  • The potential to leverage new capabilities in BigQuery for advanced analytics and data exploration.
  • A modern data infrastructure that supports the growing data needs of the business and enhances the analytics function's strategic impact.

Success Scenario

The BigQuery migration delivers a platform that not only maintains all existing analytics capabilities but enables Julianna's team to develop more sophisticated insights with less manual effort. The migration enhances her team's ability to support strategic decision-making across Vimeo, elevating their role and impact.

Presentation Strategy Tips

Emphasize BI Tool Compatibility

Directly address how Tableau and other tools she's familiar with connect to BigQuery, ideally with visual examples of the integration to demonstrate ease of transition.

Focus on Analytics Performance

Present before/after performance metrics for comparable queries and dashboards, emphasizing improved speed and capability for large-scale analytics workloads.

Address Data Reliability Concerns

Explain BigQuery's quality assurance capabilities and how the migration plan includes verification steps to ensure analytics accuracy is maintained or improved.

Demonstrate Advanced Analytics Potential

Prepare specific examples of how BigQuery ML and connected services can enhance Vimeo's analytics capabilities with minimal additional effort from her team.
