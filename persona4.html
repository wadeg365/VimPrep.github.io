Focus & Priorities

As the SVP of Data, Analytics and Integrations, Graham will be viewing the proposal from a high-level strategic and business perspective. His experience in engineering leadership, building large organizations, and driving product development means he will be interested in how the migration will enable Vimeo to achieve its business goals, empower creators and partners, and provide seamless, data-driven solutions.

He will also be concerned with the overall execution plan, potential risks, and the long-term impact on the organization. His focus is on ensuring that this migration advances Vimeo's strategic objectives and delivers tangible business value.

Key Insight

Graham's extensive experience in senior leadership roles at Microsoft and in growth-stage companies gives him a strong understanding of both enterprise-level considerations and the need for agility and innovation. His focus on multi-cloud architecture and solving hard problems suggests he will appreciate a well-thought-out and technically sound proposal with a clear strategic impact.

Watching Out For

  • The strategic rationale for migrating from Snowflake to BigQuery and how it aligns with Vimeo's overall business strategy and technology vision.
  • The benefits and value proposition of the BigQuery Lakehouse for Vimeo's creators, businesses, and partners, particularly in terms of advanced video analytics and seamless integrations.
  • The overall migration approach, timeline, and potential risks and mitigation strategies.
  • The impact on team structures and workflows across data, analytics, and integrations.
  • The scalability and future-proofing of the proposed BigQuery architecture to support Vimeo's growth and innovation.
  • The cost-effectiveness of the migration and the long-term operational costs of the new platform.
  • How the migration will enhance Vimeo's competitive advantage in the video platform market.

Potential Questions

"What is the core business driver for this migration to BigQuery, and how will it directly benefit Vimeo's strategic objectives?"

Focus on how the BigQuery Lakehouse will enable better data-driven decision making, support Vimeo's growth strategy, and provide a competitive edge through improved video analytics capabilities and integration options.

"How will the BigQuery Lakehouse specifically enhance our video analytics capabilities and enable us to provide more actionable insights to our users?"

Highlight BigQuery's strengths in processing large video metadata sets, integration with AI/ML for video content analysis, and how these capabilities translate to better features and insights for Vimeo's creators and businesses.

"Can you provide a high-level overview of the migration plan, key milestones, and potential risks, along with your proposed mitigation strategies?"

Present a clear executive summary of the migration roadmap with defined phases, key decision points, realistic timelines, and a comprehensive risk assessment with mitigation approaches for each identified risk.

"How will this migration impact our existing data, analytics, and integration teams, and what measures will be taken to ensure a smooth transition?"

Outline how teams will be supported through the transition with training, documentation, and mentoring. Present a staffing and organizational model that minimizes disruption while building new skills and capabilities.

"How does the proposed BigQuery architecture ensure the long-term scalability and flexibility required to support Vimeo's future growth and innovation?"

Explain how BigQuery's serverless architecture, separation of storage and compute, and native integration with Google Cloud's AI and ML services position Vimeo for future growth and emerging use cases in video analytics.

"What is the estimated ROI for this migration, considering both the costs and the anticipated benefits?"

Present a comprehensive ROI analysis that includes direct cost savings, productivity improvements, and business growth opportunities. Include both quantitative metrics and qualitative benefits with realistic timeframes.

Individual Wins

  • The successful implementation of a strategic data platform modernization that empowers Vimeo to deliver enhanced value to its users.
  • Improved data capabilities that drive innovation in video analytics, integrations, and overall platform connectivity.
  • A scalable and efficient data infrastructure that supports the continued growth and evolution of Vimeo.
  • The opportunity to lead a transformative initiative that strengthens Vimeo's position in the market.

Success Scenario

For Graham, success means a migration that delivers measurable business impact, strengthens Vimeo's competitive position, and creates new opportunities for product innovation and revenue growth. The project demonstrates his strategic leadership in transforming Vimeo's data capabilities and positions him as driving significant business value through technology modernization.

Presentation Strategy Tips

Lead with Business Outcomes

Start with the strategic business impact rather than technical details. Connect the BigQuery migration directly to Vimeo's business goals, revenue opportunities, and competitive positioning.

Present a Vision for Innovation

Demonstrate how BigQuery will enable new video analytics capabilities, product innovations, and integration opportunities that directly benefit Vimeo's creators and enterprise customers.

Provide a Clear Executive Roadmap

Include a concise, phase-based migration plan with clear milestones, decision points, and success metrics. Focus on how the approach minimizes business disruption while maximizing value delivery.

Emphasize ROI and Cost Efficiency

Provide a detailed financial analysis that demonstrates both short-term cost savings and long-term value creation. Include TCO comparisons between Snowflake and BigQuery with realistic assumptions.
